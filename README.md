**PlateColor** is a lightweight World of Warcraft nameplate addon built for players who want clean visuals and fast combat awareness without complex configuration. Its design focuses on **simplicity**, **low overhead**, and **practical improvements** that make nameplates easier to read in real combat.

It provides targeted customization for the most important nameplate elements, including name text, health bars, cast bars, auras, threat indicators, execute lines, target and focus markers, and personal resource display. Rather than trying to cover everything, **PlateColor** keeps its scope narrow and useful.

The addon is designed for players who prefer a **minimal setup** that works well out of the box. It avoids unnecessary menus and complicated layers of options, while still delivering the information that matters most in combat.

### 🌟 Features

* **Minimalist, lightweight design** – Optimized for performance.
* **Clean and readable** – Nameplate enhancements designed for clarity.
* **Core Support** – Health bars, cast bars, auras, threat, and markers.
* **Personal Resource Display** – Better visibility for your own resource bar.
* **Simple Setup** – No complex configuration or menu bloat.

Built by a long-time **Mythic+ player**. Features are added based on practical need and real gameplay requirements.

## 快速安装

1. 下载最新版本 [Releases](https://github.com/jianfan221/PlateColor/releases)
2. 解压到 `World of Warcraft\_retail_\Interface\AddOns\`
3. 确保文件夹名为 `PlateColor`
4. 启动游戏，在角色选择界面确认插件已加载

## 依赖

- 本插件不依赖其他插件，独立运行
- 如安装了 LibSharedMedia-3.0，可自动使用其注册的状态栏材质
